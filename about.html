My name is Paula Ellenberger and I am a graduate of Colorado State University in Fort Collins, Colorado with a BA degree in Graphic Design. As a multifaceted artist, I have passion for the visual arts, illustration, and design and have always enjoyed the visual aspects of color, form, and composition. Since 2013 I have directed my attention toward a professional career as a graphic designer and illustrator with a focus on the dreamscapes and stories of childhood. These dreamscapes stem from my own childhood imagination and my love of the natural world in which I spent my early years. This perspective gives my work a somewhat surreal aspect that echoes through my adult life as current inspiration from music and art interweave into the illustration and design style that I have created.
